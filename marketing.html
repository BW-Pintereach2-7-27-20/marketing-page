About Us

As a researcher, it's difficult to keep track of articles you want to read later. Pintereach helps you research by enabling you to save and organize articles in to categories to read later.

What does our App do?

Our app organizes the research articles for you into one place with categories. Keeps track of articles that you love and saves those articles for later. These saved articles can then be edited too.
